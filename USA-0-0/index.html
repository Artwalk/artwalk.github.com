USA-0-0

Lucky draw

In ‘15 Little Master had been writing Swift for over a year and was told to go to the WWDC session
The little mage was baffled, didn’t know what WWDC was, looked it up and clicked on a section
I was surprised to find out that even though I’m not good at English, I can make a plausible guess.
Thinking back to the days of Android tutorials, totally cloudy
So instantly filled with good feelings, went through most of the videos from that year, and benefited a lot!

Follow up to learn that with a developer account, Visa/MC credit card, you can draw a prize!
Register at the beginning of March each year at official website for one week only, so make sure you don’t miss out!

Due to the large number of people, Apple will randomly select some lucky (company reimbursement) or unlucky (self-pay) leeks to give 10,000 tickets!
After that, we’ll deduct the money whether we go or not.
No matter if the US visa is denied, denied entry, etc., the money is non-refundable.
Tickets are also forbidden to be transferred, only to other developer accounts of the same company.
Anyway, if you win the lottery, you don’t get your money back, so think carefully about the draw.

But luck was never his turn for this kind of thing, so he signed up for the no-brainer

Bingo

The results of the lottery will be announced around the end of March.

I got a $1,599 chargeback text from China Merchants Bank in the middle of the night and a stolen lock card phone call.
Too busy proving to the bank that he is himself.
I woke up the next day at noon to a confirmation email.
Busy calling the little ones and being told to hurry up with visa and hotel preparations!

Apple is offering hotels near the venue, and the price of a goose is Apple, with the cheapest costing $229 a night.
Thinking that one person is still close by, I had to make an appointment.

Luckily, one of my colleagues said he didn’t get the lottery, but he wanted to go.
Plus he’s got two buddies from Canada 🇨So_1F1E6 and So_1F1F8 from the middle of the United States who can fly to San Francisco for fun!

So checked out of the hotel and made a reservation with an Airbnb that looked pretty good.

And the little one will drive. We have a reservation for Herz.

It’s perfect.

Visa

First you have to have a passport.
Unlike the people who come up here and just get off the plane in a foreign country.
The little mage has never been anywhere before. A blank book.
Luckily, I got it when I went home to visit my parents in 15 years.

Then make a reservation at official website.
Pay online and make an appointment for an interview

Prove yourself to apply a little faster than Taobao agent!
The payment page redirects to CITIC Bank
At first glance, the page looks like a phishing site…

Interview

The imperial capital, the U.S. Embassy in Beijing, is in Sanyuanqiao, Chaoyang District.

Leave the afternoon of the interview.
When I got to the embassy, I was amazed. I’ve only seen long lines like this at the Spring Festival.
The people waiting for their visas were twisting and turning, with 10,000 zigzagging queues of people waiting for their visas.
Crawling on my belly for 3 hours before I see the window for the visa.

A total of 5-6 Chinese interview windows and 1 quiet English window.

My friend suddenly said he knew English, so he went to the English window without waiting.
The little mage thought about it and decided to give it a try.

The interviewer was a grandmother, and I can’t remember exactly what was said, but it was something along the lines of.

…
 invite us to join their conference

The old grandmother said in surprise:
Are you working for Apple?

(Little Mage thought: emmm, 2019 really, but unfortunately the current timeline is only 2016, so I had to say)
No, it’s a developer conference.

grandmother：Why they invite you?
…

Open the folder to take out the Invitation Letter.
The grandmother pointed them to the Chinese window off to the side

The security lady who kept order next to them saw that they were kicked down and asked what happened.
Sighed at this situation, but thankfully didn’t just say no and let them wait next to her for a while

Then the Chinese interview was with a foreign girl.
She’s confused, too. Conference.
Guess people who are not in the IT world don’t know what the conference is.
So she took out the invitation, and the lady said she didn’t want to read it, and proceeded to ask him to explain why

The little mage was stunned, if it might have been because of the language before, why not read the material this time as well?
I’ve only heard of people who have been rejected for not bringing documents, but I’ve never heard of people who have documents that they don’t read.
Fortunately, the partner said it was a Developer Meeting, and the lady understood in a second (😓…)

I’m not going to be able to do anything about it.

It was a long half-minute wait.
The little sister smiled and said congratulations, your visas are approved.
You’ll get your passport in the mail in a few business days.

It’s a lot of twists and turns, and I’m not particularly happy about it.
I thanked him and went home with my partner.

Then the tycoon who used to live in Boston heard about the Chinese interview.
We can both meet together.
We can even bring the whole family together.
His jaw is dropping.

Entry

Getting off the plane, entering the country

Somehow I had a vision that there were snipers lurking behind customs.
If we find the suspect, we’ll shoot him on sight.
Plus, it’s the first time I’ve set foot in another country, so I’m still a little nervous.

Customs asks the usual questions, like why you’re here, how long you’re staying.
According to the visa, it didn’t say it was a conference, it said it was a Developer Meeting.
Then customs stamped it.

The little mage took back his passport and immediately appeared calm and relaxed, but in reality, he accelerated his pace to go inside
Fear of being shot.

After seeing some of the pickups, I remembered that my little friend hadn’t come out yet.
I waited half a day, thinking my partner was GG, and then I saw him.
So the conference was checked out for a while.

Conference is really toxic 😂

Yosemite

Selected as a World Heritage Site in 1984, Yosemite is internationally recognized for its spectacular granite cliffs, waterfalls, crystal-clear streams, giant redwood forests and biodiversity.

On the way out to Yosemite, about 3 hours by car, I saw a bunch of big windmills.
Turning the corner, I suddenly saw the OS X Yosemite wallpaper.
All I’ve seen in China before are dirt mountains, but this is the first time I’ve seen this kind of bare, steep granite.
So much so that it’s a mistake to think that foreign mountains are harder than Chinese ones.

It’s incredible.

The little mage was staring at the sunset on the way back.
