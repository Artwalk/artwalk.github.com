Observer

Posted on 2016-04-03 |

It seems my observer come back.

I began to know the mental models when I was finished be friendly with the time, It took me one month to read that pure thin book.

When I was glad that my mental models improved, The Power of Now: A Guide to Spiritual Enlightenment announced that mind is a trap, the forecast ability of mind focus on the future, it prevent you to know the power of present. These different arguments confused me at that time. As I continues to read, the concept of observer appeared in my eyes.

For the first time I heard about observer was in my college. I read that word from The dark forest. It’s more fascinating after I read one book about quantum mechanics.

There is also an idea about observer in psychology. It is not only different from the idea above(Observer (quantum physics))) but also Design Patterns(Observer pattern). When I finished Think on These Things one year later, I try to build an observer about myself.
Because usually things will be more clear when you saw others doing it. The weakness and blind spot is apparent and evident, you will find better solution.

It is glad that I achieved this goal in my gap year, there was two versions of me, one acting, one watching.
I do not think the observer equals to superego, but it is clearly a guardian of ego.

But when I worked to Beijing one year and a half ago, all the works I builded ruined. The observer of me was gone. I had some new bed habits like watch the watch frequently which reveal my anxious and nervous.

When I do something wrong, I felt exhausted. Luckily I had a long vacation that I went to my parent’s home. Although that town is not an outfield, but at least it detach me from the supercity. Also, I have some deep talks about my dreams with my parents. They feels helpless because our poor family and the rising hotel rates, and they cannot understand why I’m acting so odd.

After 10 days later my vacation due, I felt relief.

Chrome to Safari

Posted on 2016-03-25 |

I use Chrome from the very beginning. I was impressed by it is simplicity and variety. Even I upgrade my laptop to Macbook, I’m still using it because of three main reasons

  • Plugin
  • Shortcut
  • Develop
  • Flash

But after my TL amway me, I tried Safari deeply, and found:

Plugin

  • Adblock Plus
  • 1Password
  • Evernote
  • AutoPagerize
  • Clean Links for Google

Shortcut

I can use opt+cmd+> on Chrome to change tabs, it is shift+cmd+[ on Safari
shift+cmd+z to unclose the most recent closed tabs, it’s no corresponding key on Safari, I can only use history instead.

Develop

I used to opt+cmd+i in chrome to simulate iPhone, I can compare iOS version with front end in this way.
When I got to know what’s safari’s Develop look like, (cmd+, -> Advanced -> Show Develop menu in menu bar or opt+cmd+r) I surrendered.

Flash

Finally, I accept that different device have their own place. So as for most Chinese video Website still using flash, I chose chrome to watch them.
