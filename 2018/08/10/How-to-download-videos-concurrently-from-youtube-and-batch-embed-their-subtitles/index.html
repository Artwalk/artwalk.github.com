How to download videos concurrently from youtube and batch embed their subtitles

LeiHao's Blog
2018-08-10
ffmpeg, youtube

The whole thing I want to do is to download some courses which help me a lot on youtube and embed Zh-Hans subtitle to the video then submit them to a Chinese website which does not support external subtitle.

This thing may help the students in China who cannot speak English well and cannot access youtube.

Youtube-dl config

The file path is ~/.config/youtube-dl/config

Download concurrently

Batch translate filenames

After downloading all the videos in the list, get their names with

ls | grep mkv | sed "s/\.mkv//"

then copy & paste to google to get the translated text, then save it to a zh.txt

Batch embed subtitles

It usually took me a whole night for 20 videos on Intel Core i7, and an hour video needs 20 minutes to embed its subtitle.
