2013-11-17 11:40:48

I watched a video of Xiaoxin Chen before practice.

I didn’t think Dantian is exist, because it was not scientific.
But it seems something true on my resent experience.

Ignoring traditional Chinese view of it(sea of Qi), the lower dantian seems like the center of balance and gravity.
And I also found the same theory on wikipedia.

Here is a simple way to how take advantage of it:

  1. Close your eyes
  2. Simplify your body in to a matchstick men
  3. Imaging your eyes in the lower dantian(which means you are in your gravity center)
  4. practice

notice:
You’ll find that you’re hardly to STAND ON ONE LEG WITH EYES CLOSED, but it’s OK after a few days.
And if you have no problem with that, congratulations! You have a tremendous balance talent.

2013-11-10

I found Chen-style when I was in college, Studied the new frame.

Nowadays I really feels something, is that QI?
I practices it for two weeks, because my back and neck were pain again.

I always want to finish the frame in a little time, especially as a young man, but

Empty yourself of everything. Maintain a steady serenity.
——Tao Teh Ching 16

I know I should feel my body’s change. Moving in a very slowly way is one mothed.

In the process, my body become warm, and my bones make popping sounds, which was fun.
And I can feels my spine was corrected.
I can’t wait to see what will happen to me after one month.
