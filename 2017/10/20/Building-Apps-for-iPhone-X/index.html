Building Apps for iPhone X

LeiHao's Blog
2017-10-20
iOS

First, Watching Building Apps for iPhone X with 2x speed to get a glance, it will speed 13/2 minutes.

  • The critical point is to open the Safe Area in Storyboard.
  • The LayoutConstraint attracted to Superview can beyond the Safe Area.

My current app has 17 Storyboards and 28 ViewControllers.

In each ViewController, the previous developers used an UIView on top to cover the system’s navigationController to do some tricks, which means the constraint of the view has a height == 64, and top == superview.top.

Although it’s a little disgusting, it works on previous devices in which the navigation bar’s height is always 64.

But on iPhone X, it needs to use Safe Area. Which means I have better to delete the fake navigation view. But that means it moves all the buttons, actions, animations to the system’s navigationController if the segue kind is push.

Honestly, to say, it’s much easier than I thought after all the works are done.

For the segue kind is present modally, the fake view still does its job by changed it’s height only if the device is iPhone X.
