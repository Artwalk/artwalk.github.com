Three-year round-trip visa

Unlike the U.S. visa, it is not possible to make a reservation by yourself, but only through the designated travel agency.

  • After tax 25W+
  • Taobao mailing of documents such as passports and tax forms

The whole thing was in the mail. I thought I’d lose it.
I didn’t think it would be done in two weeks.

Business Class

On most Asian airlines, business class and first class are the same.

This Boeing 787, for example, has four seats in one row. 💺
Windows on both sides, no neighbors, couple’s seat in the middle.
1 Area occupied by business class seats == 6 Economy class

It’s an All Nippon Airways promotion, so it’s still a good deal.

Hot towels, champagne and a variety of beverages provided on board
Lunch can be reserved in advance for Japanese or Western cuisine.
The dinnerware was replaced with heavy china plates and metal knives and forks. 🍴

The best part is that the seat can be flattened to 180 degrees at the push of a small button.
WC is no longer a constant stream of people.
The children who touched it were polite and didn’t cry the whole way.

The engine roars a lot less, and the default headphones are the Bose QC 25.

Because the capital is only 3 hours away from Tokyo.

The basic process is
Have a meal and a drink on the plane.
Read your name.
Lie down and get some sleep.
We’re almost there.

The overall experience is awesome.

But the back of business class is empty.
I was worried. I’ll go to Japan next time.
All Nippon Airways isn’t going out of business, is it?

Viewing platform

You can watch planes take off and land and feel the engine roar more realistically than an IMAX!
