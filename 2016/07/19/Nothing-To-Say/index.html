Nothing To Say

Artwalk's Blog
2016-07-19
Self

It’s been a long time that I didn’t write anything. Maybe written in English fully is the obstacle.

Too many things happened in these two months.

First, my company failed in series D funding round.

Our CTO had to reduce the design apartment’s programmers by more than a third and reduce three apps to one. Almost half of the programmers were leave. It seems everything went back to B round happy time.
But, something never went back again.

My project iterated to version 1.6 and be cut. So, there is no more coding job, after that, I went to San Francisco to join WWDC16.

Second, Do what you love.

So, programming is my job, exact to iOS developer after my gap year.

I subscribed many Tech blog when I were work in the beginning, but they never rose my attention again. At the same time, I lost every interest of everything, and don’t want to meet anyone.

It may I have no real hobby cause that, the only former interest was programming.

But finally, I have a little luxury time to relax myself both physically and mentally after I went to Beijing two years ago which was the most suffocating super city in the world.

Third, I did resize how important of the excise when I came back from SFO. Almost every elitist do excise in developed country.

After I did see the blue sky every where, the wide of Pacific Ocean, the green leaf in the sunshine, the house on the hill covered in mist.
I know there is a real better world in the world which is waiting for me.
Keep myself heathy is in top priority, all other things are secondary.
